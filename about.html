The Indian Basketball Association (IBA) at Purdue University was founded with a simple yet powerful goal: to bring together students who share a passion for basketball and create a vibrant, competitive, and inclusive community.

We organize seasonal tournaments that provide students with opportunities to compete, stay active, and build lasting friendships. Whether you're a seasoned player or just getting started, IBA welcomes everyone to join the game.
