About IBA

Building community and competition through basketball at Purdue University

Our Mission

The Indian Basketball Association (IBA) at Purdue University was founded with a straightforward goal: to bring together students who share a passion for basketball and create a vibrant, competitive, and inclusive community.

We organize seasonal tournaments that give students meaningful opportunities to compete, stay active, and build lasting connections. Whether you're an experienced player or picking up the game for the first time, IBA welcomes everyone to the court.

What We Offer

  • Competitive Tournaments: Organized seasonal play with structured brackets, full schedules, and championship games
  • Fair Play: A seeding system built around team performance and demonstrated skill level
  • Community: A network of students who share a genuine love for the game
  • Skill Development: Consistent, high-quality competition that pushes every player to improve
  • Flexible Participation: Register as a free agent or form your own team — both paths are supported
  • Real-Time Operations: Live brackets, game schedules, and stat tracking available online

How It Works

1
Registration Sign up as an individual or register your team before the season begins.

4

Get Involved

  • Join a Team: Sign up and connect with an existing team that needs players
  • Start a Team: Recruit your friends and register as team captain
  • Follow Along: Stay informed on Instagram, YouTube, and GroupMe for schedules and highlights
  • Volunteer: Help run events, referee games, or assist with tournament logistics
